Lafayette, Louisiana

Lafayette is a beautiful city! It's just the right size, too. Not too big, not too small. For that reason, it's (usually) pretty easy to get around, and there's plenty to do. We have sports, scenic views, all kinds of entertainment, and (as all Louisiana cities have) GREAT food!

Here's some of my favorite places in Lafayette (click them to find out more):

  • University of Louisiana at Lafayette (my alma mater!)
  • Reve Coffee Shops
  • Acadian Village (I got married here!)
  • Lafayette Farmers and Artisans Market
